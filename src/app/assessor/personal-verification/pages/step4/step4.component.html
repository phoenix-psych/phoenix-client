Please ensure that you have logged into the Associate AIMS page. Here you will be able to upload the following documents to support your Associate Registration. Please ensure that you click on the 'Next' button on the AIMS portal to see the full range of upload possibilities

You will be required to upload the following documents (that are applicable to your chosen Associate area):

SpLD - Specialist Assessors/Teachers/Coaches:

  1. Level 7 or above qualification/certificate
  2. Specialist Teaching Qualification (SpLD or other)
  3. AAT/CCET Certificate 
  4. Practising Certificate
  5. Coaching CPD certificates or qualifications
  6. If you have other memberships not mentioned in the Associate Registration form, please provide evidence of these

Psychologists (Occupational):

  1. British Psychological Society Membership
  2. Health Care Professional Council registration
  3. If you have other memberships not mentioned in the Associate Registration form, please provide evidence of these

Counsellor:

  1. BACP Membership
  2. The National Counselling Society
  3. If you have other memberships not mentioned in the Associate Registration form, please provide evidence of these

Other Documentation - Mandatory:

  1. Recent CRB or enhanced CRB document
  2. Professional Indemnity Insurance Certificate
  3. ICO certificate - confirmation (document/email including ICO registration number)
  4. Right To Work in the UK - Copy of document you are providing as evidence for your right to work in the UK
  5. Copy of your current Curriculum Vitae with recent CPD or training attended where possible
